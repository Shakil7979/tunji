About me

I'm the founder of Scantranx, an award-winning Canadian retail technology company. My dedication to the success of small to mid-sized retailers led me to found Scantranx, with the goal of competing with large retail chains by providing an exceptional and distinctive customer experience.

With a rich background as a software engineer, I've led various projects in the Financial, Retail, and eCommerce sectors. This experience inspired me to create a cloud-based omnichannel retail software, tailored to empower small and medium-sized retailers for accelerated business growth.Scantranx was honored with the Intel award for best software idea, and I was granted the Canada Start-up visa by the Government of Canada in recognition of my innovation.

I had the privilege to speak at the 2020 International eCommerce and Omnichannel Transformation Conference, and I was part of the Canadian Delegation to the G20 Young Entrepreneurs Alliance Summit (YEA) where I played a role in influencing G20 policies and representing the interests of young entrepreneurs from across G20 nations.

My educational background includes a Bachelor of Science degree (Honors) in Computing and Its Practice from The Open University in the United Kingdom, as well as various significant certifications in Information Technology.

During my free time, I enjoy watching or playing football, engaging in family activities, and grilling some barbecue turkey wings.
